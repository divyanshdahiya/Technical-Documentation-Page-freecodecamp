Ingredients and Equipment
If you're using unground coffee, please coarsely grind them to resemble that of sea salt first. Also please note that 1 tbsp / 5 grams of ground coffee is recommanded per cup.
  • Ground coffee (Medium roast Guatemalan or Columbian is preffered)
  • Cold water and some ice
  • Milk or almond milk
  • French Press (Cafetiere) and measuring spoons or kitchen scales

Preparations
You will need 1oz / 30ml of water per 1tbsp / 5g of coffee.
  • Put the desired amount of coffee into the French press.
  • Add water to wet the grounds and cover but Do not plunge it yet.

Brewing
If your fridge is small or does not have enough headroom, you can use cling film or similar cover instead.
  • Place the cafetiere in the fridge.
  • Let it cold brew for 16 - 20 hours.

One last step
Make sure you pour coffee over ice, not coffee, followed by ice!
  • Gently stir first.
  • Slowly press the plunger.
  • Pour over a glass/mug of ice.

Serve
Almond is a great non-dairy/vegan alternative. Coconut is nice too but soy/rice/hemp aren't recommanded.
  • Add milk or almond milk to taste.
  • A little more water can be added if your cold brew is too strong for your liking. Make it yours!
